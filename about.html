About

On arrival

UPON ARRIVAL AT THE FARMHOUSE, YOU WILL BE GREETED BY ONE OF OUR ESTEEMED HOSTS AND LED TO YOUR TABLE.

Whether you choose to dine within our luxurious dining room, adorned with marble tables and velvet chairs, or opt for a more alfresco experience in our beautifully landscaped gardens, complete with state-of-the-art outdoor water feature and Indian sandstone, you will be enveloped in elegance.

Our outdoor dining area, complete with heaters for those chillier nights, offers a stunning view of the Kashmiri-inspired scenery. At The Farmhouse, we have created a dining experience unlike any other, inviting you to indulge in the finest of culinary delights in the most refined of settings.

Award winning

AT THE FARMHOUSE, WE PRIDE OURSELVES ON OFFERING A DINING EXPERIENCE THAT IS TRULY UNPARALLELED.

Our award-winning culinary team has meticulously crafted a menu that is a true feast for the senses, featuring a selection of genuinely mouth-watering dishes that blend the highest quality flavours from around the globe into a one-of-a-kind culinary masterpiece.

In addition to our exquisite fare, our expert mixologists have prepared a range of non-alcoholic and mocktail beverages to complement your meal. Whether you are seeking a sophisticated evening out or a casual designer dining experience, The Farmhouse is the perfect choice for all your culinary needs.
