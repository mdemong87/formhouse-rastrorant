Welcome

To The Farmhouse

Nestled in the heart of Coventry, The Farmhouse has flourished over the past decade as a beacon of culinary excellence. Our award-winning street food, bursting with explosive flavours, elevates the humble dishes of soul food to new heights, tantalising the taste buds with each bite. The Farmhouses sleek and sophisticated surroundings, paired with impeccable service, provide the perfect setting for any occasion be it a unique culinary adventure, a romantic date, or a celebration with loved ones.

As a restaurant that is not only changing the game, but inspiring a new generation of eateries, we invite you to join us at The Farmhouse and discover the unmatched excellence that has made us a true success.
